## Decision Criteria

Promote a logion or subunit to `INCLUDE_WITH_MARKER` only if:

- the early core can be stated cleanly without a late expansion;
- the core has strong external control or strong textual support;
- the unit does not merely duplicate an already included saying;
- the Thomas-specific ending, frame, or theological expansion can be left out without damaging the core;
- the result improves the reconstructed sayings-gospel rather than simply making it longer.

Keep as `UNCERTAIN` or split-core appendix material if:

- the unit is mainly controlled by one late-looking source;
- the Thomas form is too dependent on a recognizable gospel redaction;
- the core duplicates a better already-included form;
- the secondary ending is too entangled with the core;
- the unit requires more cluster-level work before printing.
