# Logion 79: Womb, Word, and Eschatological Reversal Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 79 combines a blessing of the womb/breasts of Jesus' mother, a response blessing those who hear and keep the Father's word, and a later saying about barren wombs and breasts that did not nurse.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Luke | 11:27-28 | Blessing of womb/breasts redirected to hearing and keeping God's word. |
| Luke | 23:29 | Blessing of barren wombs and breasts that did not nurse. |

## Assessment

The first subunit has strong control. The full Thomas sequence is composite because it joins two Lukan-like settings.

## Reconstruction Implication

Consider only the hearing/keeping word core after further study. Do not include the whole logion.
