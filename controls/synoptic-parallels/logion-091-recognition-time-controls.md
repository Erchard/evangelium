# Logion 91: Recognition of the Time Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 91 combines two elements:

1. a dialogue frame: "Tell us who you are so that we may believe";
2. a recognition rebuke: people read the face of sky and earth but do not recognize the one before them or this time.

Only the second element is a possible early core candidate.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Luke | 12:54-56 | Strong control for reading cloud/wind/weather but not interpreting the present time. |
| Matthew | 16:2-3 | Strong control for reading the face of the sky but not the signs of the times. |
| Thomas | Logion 5 / hidden-revealed cluster | Internal thematic control for failure to recognize what is present. |

## Split-Core Assessment

Possible early core:

> You know how to read the face of sky and earth, but you do not recognize this time.

The core has good synoptic control and may preserve an early recognition-of-time warning.

The dialogue opening should not be included in the clean reader. It is a christological recognition frame and makes the full Thomas logion more developed than the likely core.

## Greek Policy

No loaded Greek Oxyrhynchus Thomas witness preserves Logion 91. Any Greek form in the project must be labeled `Greek retroversion, hypothetical`.

Canonical Greek controls may be used for comparison, but they are not Thomas manuscript witnesses.

## Reconstruction Implication

Do not add the full logion to the clean reader.

Promote only the recognition-of-time core to a later controlled clean-reader candidate pass, where it must be tested against duplication with already included hidden/revealed and recognition sayings.
