# Logion 95: Money, Interest, and Non-Repayment Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 95 says not to lend money at interest, but to give to someone from whom repayment will not be received.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Luke | 6:34-35 | Strong control for lending without expecting return. |
| Deuteronomy | 23:19-20 | Jewish legal background for anti-interest teaching. |
| Leviticus | 25:35-37 | Anti-usury and support for the poor. |
| Psalm | 15:5 | Wisdom/ethical background against lending at interest. |

## Assessment

The ethical core is plausible and important. The logion combines anti-usury law and radical non-repayment giving, so the final decision should test whether this is one early unit or a secondary ethical combination.

## Reconstruction Implication

Possible marked ethical-core candidate. Compare with Logion 6 before reader inclusion.
