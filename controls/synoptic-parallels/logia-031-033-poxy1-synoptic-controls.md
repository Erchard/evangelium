# Logia 31-33: P.Oxy. 1 and Synoptic Controls

Status: evidence table v0.1.

## Purpose

This table tests Thomas 31-33 as high-candidate units after the P.Oxy. 1 corpus completion sprint.

## Thomas Witnesses

| Logion | Coptic status | Greek P.Oxy. 1 status | Main issue |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| 31 | Complete | Strong: prophet + physician clauses preserved | Prophet core is strongly paralleled; physician clause may be a paired expansion. |
| 32 | Complete | Strong: city on high mountain preserved | Very close to Matt 5:14, with Thomas/P.Oxy. "established/fortified" expansion. |
| 33 | Complete | Very partial: opening only | Ear/rooftop opening has Greek support; lamp section has Coptic + synoptic controls but no extant Greek Thomas in P.Oxy. 1. |

## Synoptic Controls

| Thomas unit | Main controls | Assessment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 31a prophet in homeland | Mark 6:4; Matthew 13:57; Luke 4:24 | Very strong. A short proverbial Jesus tradition is widely attested. |
| 31b physician clause | Luke 4:23; Mark 2:17 and parallels as physician motif | Thematic and contextual controls; not a direct parallel to the Thomas physician clause. |
| 32 city on mountain | Matthew 5:14 | Very strong. Thomas/P.Oxy. has a fuller form with "built/established." |
| 33a ear/rooftops | Matthew 10:27; Luke 12:3 | Strong. Thomas compresses hearing and public proclamation. |
| 33b lamp/lampstand | Mark 4:21; Matthew 5:15; Luke 8:16; Luke 11:33 | Very strong synoptic support. Greek Thomas witness is not extant for this part in P.Oxy. 1. |

## Reconstruction Implication

All three units may be included with markers:

- 31: include prophet core; retain physician clause as marked paired expansion.
- 32: include city-on-mountain saying with marker for "built/established."
- 33: include as a split marked unit: ear/rooftops plus lamp/lampstand.

None should be promoted to unmarked `INCLUDE` before final publication-level collation.
