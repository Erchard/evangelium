# Logion 73: Harvest and Workers

Status: synoptic control table v0.1.

## Short Conclusion

Thomas 73 is a strong short-form candidate for `INCLUDE_WITH_MARKER`. It closely parallels Matthew 9:37-38 and Luke 10:2, and it lacks obvious Thomasine expansion.

## Reconstruction Decision

Decision: `INCLUDE_WITH_MARKER`.

Reason: the saying is brief, formulaic, and doubly controlled by Matthew and Luke. It remains marked because no loaded Greek Thomas witness preserves it.

## Direction of Dependence

Thomas 73 could preserve a mobile mission saying that Matthew and Luke place in mission-discourse contexts. It could also be a Thomasine extraction from a synoptic mission context. Because Matthew and Luke are both close, the saying almost certainly circulated in Greek. The lack of distinctive Thomasine expansion makes the core especially strong, though not unmarked.

## Possible Early Core

> The harvest is great, but the workers are few; ask the Lord of the harvest to send workers into the harvest.
