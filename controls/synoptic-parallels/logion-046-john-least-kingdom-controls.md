# Logion 46: John the Baptist and the Least in the Kingdom Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 46 states that none born of women is greater than John the Baptist, but the one who becomes small among the disciples will know the kingdom and become greater than John.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Matthew | 11:11 | None born of women greater than John; least in the kingdom greater than he. |
| Luke | 7:28 | Very close parallel to Matthew's John/kingdom contrast. |
| Thomas | 22 | Internal control for small children / small ones and kingdom. |

## Assessment

The John/least-in-kingdom core is strong. Thomas's "know the kingdom" wording may be secondary or Thomasine.
