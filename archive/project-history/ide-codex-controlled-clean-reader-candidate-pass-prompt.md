## Strict Inclusion Criteria

Promote a candidate only if it is:

- short or cleanly extractable;
- not already represented by a stronger included logion;
- not dependent on a late or highly specific narrative frame;
- supported by strong external controls or a notably compact Thomas form;
- printable without importing a large speculative reconstruction.
