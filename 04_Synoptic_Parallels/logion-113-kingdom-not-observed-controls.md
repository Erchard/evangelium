# Logion 113: Kingdom Not Observed Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 113 says the kingdom does not come by observation, will not be located as "here" or "there," and is spread out upon the earth though people do not see it.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Luke | 17:20-21 | Strong control for not-by-observation and not here/there. |
| Thomas | 3 | Internal control for kingdom not located here or there. |

## Assessment

The not-observed/not-here-or-there core is strong, but the "spread out upon the earth" ending is distinctly Thomasine and should be marked.

## Reconstruction Implication

Possible marked-core candidate, but decide together with Logion 3 to avoid unnecessary duplication in the clean reader.
