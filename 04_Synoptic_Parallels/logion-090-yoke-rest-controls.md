# Logion 90: Yoke and Rest Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 90 invites hearers to come to Jesus because his yoke is good, his lordship is gentle, and they will find rest.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Matthew | 11:28-30 | Very close control: come, yoke, gentle/easy burden, rest. |

## Assessment

The control is strong but narrow. Because Matthew is the main canonical control, Logion 90 is weaker for earliest-layer reconstruction than sayings with independent double or multiple controls.

## Reconstruction Implication

Keep outside the clean reader for now. Treat as appendix material unless a later decision pass accepts a marked Matthean-control unit.
