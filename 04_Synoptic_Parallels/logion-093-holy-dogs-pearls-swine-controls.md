# Logion 93: Holy Things, Dogs, Pearls, and Swine Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 93 warns not to give what is holy to dogs and not to throw pearls to swine.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Matthew | 7:6 | Very close holy/dogs and pearls/swine control. |
| Thomas internal | Secrecy and hiddenness motifs | Controls the collectional function of guarded revelation. |

## Assessment

The proverb-like core may be early, but the one-source Matthean control and Thomas's secrecy setting weaken confidence for clean-reader inclusion.

## Reconstruction Implication

Keep outside the clean reader. Use in the appendix to explain guarded-revelation material and the danger of mistaking thematic fit for earliest-layer certainty.
