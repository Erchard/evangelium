## Main Evidence

Logion 47 chains several sayings:

- two horses / two bows;
- no servant can serve two masters;
- old and new wine;
- new wine and old wineskins;
- old patch and new garment.

The two-masters and wine/patch units have strong synoptic controls. The two-horses/two-bows opening may be a Thomasine or independent introductory analogy.

## Early-Core Assessment

Most plausible early cores:

> No servant can serve two masters.

and:

> New wine is not put into old wineskins.

The patch saying is also a plausible traditional unit, but it should be evaluated separately.

## Secondary-Layer Indicators

The full Thomas sequence is a collected chain of related incompatibility sayings. That arrangement may be secondary even if several individual units are early.

## Limits
