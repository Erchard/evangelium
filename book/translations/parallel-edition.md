# Parallel Edition

Status: publication-polished parallel edition v0.8; Greek-layer publication polish checked 2026-07-18.

This table aligns the current clean Ukrainian reader with compact Coptic, Greek, English, and decision layers. It is a working parallel edition, not a full critical apparatus. Full Coptic forms are kept in `reconstructed-gospel-coptic.md`; fuller Greek notes are kept in `reconstructed-gospel-greek.md`.

## Witness Labels

- `Extant Greek witness`: preserved in a loaded Greek Thomas papyrus witness.
- `Partial extant Greek witness`: only part of the unit is preserved in a loaded Greek Thomas papyrus witness.
- `Lacunose extant Greek`: preserved or reconstructed in a Greek papyrus witness with major lacunae.
- `Greek retroversion, hypothetical`: a proposed Greek back-translation; not a manuscript witness.
