# Logion 96: Leaven Parable Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 96 compares the kingdom of the Father to a woman who hides a little leaven in dough and makes large loaves, ending with a hearing formula.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Matthew | 13:33 | Strong kingdom/leaven/woman/dough control. |
| Luke | 13:20-21 | Strong kingdom/leaven/woman/flour control. |

## Assessment

The parable core is strong. Thomas's "kingdom of the Father" frame and final hearing formula may be collectional or secondary.

## Reconstruction Implication

Possible future marked parable core. Do not include the frame and hearing formula without separate argument.
