# Logion 99: True Family Controls

Status: working control file v0.1.

## Thomas Unit

Thomas 99 redefines Jesus' family as those who do the will of his Father and adds that they will enter the kingdom of his Father.

## Controls

| Source | Passage | Control value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mark | 3:31-35 | Family outside; true family as those who do God's will. |
| Matthew | 12:46-50 | Close control for doing the will of the Father. |
| Luke | 8:19-21 | Related form: those who hear and do the word of God. |

## Assessment

The true-family core is strong. The kingdom-of-the-Father ending is not required by the core and should remain marked.

## Reconstruction Implication

Possible split-core candidate. Do not include the kingdom ending without separate argument.
