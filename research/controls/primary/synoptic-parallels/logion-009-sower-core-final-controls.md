# Logion 9: Sower Core Final Controls

Статус: фінальна контрольна таблиця v0.1 для рішення щодо короткого ядра.

## Decision

Short sower core: `INCLUDE_WITH_MARKER`.

Full Thomas 9: `UNCERTAIN`.

Greek status: `Greek retroversion, hypothetical`; no loaded P.Oxy. witness preserves Thomas 9.

## Reconstructed Core

English:

> A sower went out to sow. Some seed fell on the road, some on rock, some among thorns, and some on good earth; only the good earth bore fruit.

Ukrainian:

> Вийшов сіяч сіяти. Одне насіння впало на дорогу, інше на камінь, інше між терня, інше на добру землю; тільки добра земля дала плід.

This is not the full Coptic text of Thomas 9. It is a marked reconstruction of the short non-allegorical parable core.

## Rationale

The short parable core has strong multiple synoptic control and is independently intelligible without the later allegorical interpretations. Thomas 9 is valuable because it preserves the parable without explaining the seed as the word or the soils as classes of hearers.

The full Coptic Thomas form remains uncertain. The worm, the "upward to heaven" phrase, and the `60 / 120` yield may preserve local tradition, Thomasine reshaping, translation effects, or secondary expansion. They should remain in the apparatus until further study.
