# Logia 36 and 39: P.Oxy. 655 and Synoptic Controls

Status: evidence table v0.1.

## Purpose

This table tests the two strongest candidates from the P.Oxy. 655 block after the primary corpus sprint: Logion 36 and Logion 39.

## Thomas and Related Witnesses

| Unit | Coptic Thomas | Greek P.Oxy. 655 | Additional control | Main issue |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 36 anxiety / clothing | Complete, short, focused on clothing | Extant but heavily restored; fuller complex with food, clothing, lilies, adding to stature/life | P.Oxy. 5575 preserves related Matthew/Luke/Thomas-like material in a mixed sayings environment | Need separate short clothing core from wider food/lilies complex. |
| 39a keys of knowledge | Complete | Extant but heavily restored; key(s) of knowledge / not entering / blocking entry | Luke 11:52; Matthew 23:13 | Strong external controls, but anti-scribal polemic may be shaped by later community conflict. |
| 39b serpents and doves | Complete | Extant but heavily restored near the end | Matthew 10:16 | Strong control, but its attachment to 39a may be editorial. |

## Synoptic Controls

| Thomas unit | Main controls | Assessment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 36 short anxiety/clothing core | Matthew 6:25, 6:28-30; Luke 12:22, 12:27-28 | Strong. The Thomas Coptic form is shorter than Matthew/Luke and P.Oxy. 655, but the anti-anxiety/clothing motif is well controlled. |
| 36 wider food/lilies complex | Matthew 6:25-34; Luke 12:22-31; P.Oxy. 5575 | Strong as a circulating complex, but too fluid to print as a full early Thomas reconstruction without markers. |
| 39a keys of knowledge / not entering | Luke 11:52; Matthew 23:13 | Very strong. Luke is especially close because it has "key of knowledge"; Matthew controls the not-entering / blocking-entry structure. |
| 39b wise serpents / pure doves | Matthew 10:16 | Very strong verbal control. Thomas/P.Oxy. 655 likely preserves the same short wisdom-mission saying. |

## Reconstruction Implication

Logion 36 may be included with a marker as a short anti-anxiety saying focused on clothing. The fuller food/lilies/stature complex should remain in the apparatus.

Logion 39 may be included with markers only as split units:

- a marked polemical saying about the key(s) of knowledge;
- a marked wisdom saying about serpents and doves.

The current Thomas form should not be printed as an unmarked single composition.
