# Final Reader Probability-Pressure Review v0.1

Date: 2026-07-18

## Purpose

This review follows `project/probability-vs-reader-decision-audit-2026-07-18.md`.

It asks whether the current clean reader is distorted by probability tension:

- a late-leaning logion included in the clean reader;
- early-leaning logia excluded from the clean reader;
- duplicate or unclear probability profiles that make the project look internally inconsistent.

This review does not change the clean reader. It defines what must be checked before final freeze.

## Controlling Principle

Raw early probability does not equal clean-reader inclusion.

A saying may have an early traditional core and still remain outside the clean reader if the preserved Thomas form is composite, dependent-risky, redundant with a stronger included unit, or not yet safely split from a later frame.

## Logion 1: Included Late-Pressure Case

| Logion | Early | Late | Current reader | Review outcome |
| ---: | ---: | ---: | --- | --- |
| 1 | 35 | 65 | YES | `MOVE_TO_FRAME_REVIEW_BEFORE_FINAL_FREEZE` |

Logion 1 is not a normal aphorism or parable in the same sense as most clean-reader units. It functions as a collection frame: whoever finds the interpretation of these sayings will not taste death.

Reasons it may remain in the project:

- It is integral to the received sayings collection.
- It has P.Oxy. 654 Greek control, though with restored lacunae.
- It tells the reader how the collection expects to be read.

Reasons it is risky in the clean reconstructed text:

- Its current latest probability profile leans late: early 35, late 65.
- It is probably a hermeneutical prologue or collectional frame, not an independent earliest saying.
- If printed as Logion 1 without explanation, it may weaken the claim that the first layer is the earliest recoverable sayings gospel.

Recommendation:

- Do not remove it in this review.
- Before final freeze, decide whether the clean reader should have:
  - a separate unnumbered / framed prologue section for Logion 1;
  - Logion 1 retained as numbered text but explicitly treated in apparatus as collection-frame material;
  - Logion 1 moved out of the clean reconstructed sayings text and into the appendix / introduction.

Current preferred direction:

- Treat Logion 1 as a `FRAME_REVIEW` item, not as an ordinary saying.
