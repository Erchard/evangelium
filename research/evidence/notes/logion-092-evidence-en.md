## Decision

Current publication decision: `DEFER_TO_CLUSTER`.

Reader implication: do not include Logion 92 as a full saying in the clean reconstruction.

## Source Basis

- Coptic base: extant in NHC II,2.
- P.Oxy. status: no loaded Oxyrhynchus Greek witness.
- Greek policy: the opening seek/find wording may be retroverted with some confidence, but the full saying remains hypothetical.
- Canonical controls: Matthew 7:7 and Luke 11:9 for seek/find.
- Internal controls: Logia 2 and 94.

## Rationale

The opening "seek and you will find" is a strong short core, but the rest of the logion looks like internal collection memory: things once asked, not then told, now offered, no longer sought.

Because the clean reader already contains Logion 2 as the project's main search/find unit, this full saying should be deferred rather than duplicated or promoted.
