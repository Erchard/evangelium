## Main Evidence

Thomas 46 has a close structural relationship to Matthew 11:11 and Luke 7:28: John the Baptist is greatest among those born of women, yet another category related to the kingdom surpasses him.

Thomas differs by saying that the one who becomes small among the disciples will know the kingdom and become greater than John.

## Early-Core Assessment

Possible early core:

> Among those born of women none is greater than John; yet the least/small one in the kingdom is greater than he.

This core is strongly controlled by Matthew and Luke.

## Secondary-Layer Indicators

Thomas's "know the kingdom" wording may be a Thomasine reshaping. The Adam-to-John frame may also be secondary or interpretive.

## Limits

No extant Greek Thomas witness is loaded. The relationship between Thomas, Matthew, Luke, and shared tradition cannot be resolved yet.
