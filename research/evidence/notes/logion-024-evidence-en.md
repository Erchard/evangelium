# Logion 24 Evidence Note

Status: evidence rationale v0.1.

## Decision

`DEFER`; keep outside the clean reader.

## Working Unit

> The disciples ask for Jesus' place; Jesus answers with the light inside a person of light.

## Textual Witnesses

- Coptic base: extant NHC II,2; primary preserved witness for the light saying.
- P.Oxy. 1: not preserved in the loaded corpus.
- P.Oxy. 654: not preserved; this papyrus covers the prologue and Logia 1-7.
- P.Oxy. 655: catalogued as Thomas 24(?), 36-39, but the loaded line-level Greek does not preserve the Coptic light saying.
- Canonical controls: Matthew 5:14-16 and Luke 11:34-36 are thematic light controls, not Thomas witnesses.

## Main Evidence

The Coptic form is a coherent light/location saying. P.Oxy. 655 is important, but its extant line-level content aligns with the appearance/undressing material treated as Logion 37, not with the Coptic light saying of Logion 24. Therefore the P.Oxy. 655 association must remain possible/uncertain.

## Reason For Non-Inclusion

The saying is attractive but source-critical risk is high. Treating P.Oxy. 655 as direct Greek support for the light saying would overstate the evidence. The Coptic light language may also reflect developed Thomasine anthropology.

## Reconstruction Implication

Do not print Logion 24 in the clean reader. In the apparatus, separate:

1. the Coptic light saying;
2. the P.Oxy. 655 Thomas 24(?) catalogue issue;
3. the Logion 37 appearance/undressing Greek material.

## Remaining Work

Create a dedicated P.Oxy. 655 / Thomas 24(?) control note if the project later revisits whether any part of Logion 24 can be reconstructed as an early core.
