# Logion 37 Evidence Note

Status: evidence rationale v0.1.

## Decision

`UNCERTAIN`; keep outside the clean reader.

## Working Unit

> When will Jesus appear? When you undress and are not ashamed.

## Textual Witnesses

- Coptic base: extant NHC II,2; preserves a fuller form with clothing under feet, children, seeing the Son of the Living One, and fearlessness.
- P.Oxy. 1: not preserved.
- P.Oxy. 654: not preserved.
- P.Oxy. 655: extant Greek witness for the shorter appearance/undressing/no-shame core.
- Canonical controls: no close synoptic control established.
- Cluster control: unity/children/nakedness cluster.

## Main Evidence

P.Oxy. 655 provides genuine Greek support for the shorter core. The Coptic form expands the imagery substantially. This difference is important: the Greek witness strengthens the logion, but not necessarily the full Coptic expansion.

## Reason For Non-Inclusion

The project has not yet determined whether the shorter Greek core is early enough for inclusion. The fuller Coptic form strongly fits Thomasine symbolic language and should remain apparatus material unless separately justified.

## Reconstruction Implication

Do not print Logion 37 in the clean reader at this stage. Mark it as a serious future split-core candidate.

## Remaining Work

Study nakedness/no-shame, child imagery, and Son-of-the-Living-One language separately. Any future reader proposal should consider only the shorter Greek-supported core first.
