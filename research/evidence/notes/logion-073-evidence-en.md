## Main Evidence

Thomas 73 preserves the short harvest/laborers formula:

> The harvest is great, but the workers are few; ask the Lord to send workers into the harvest.

Matthew and Luke preserve very close forms. The saying is compact, memorable, and mission-oriented.

## Early-Core Assessment

Possible early core:

> The harvest is plentiful, the workers few; ask the Lord of the harvest to send workers.

This is a strong early-saying candidate due to brevity, multiple attestation, and lack of complex Thomasine symbolism.

## Secondary-Layer Indicators

The saying may have been shaped by early mission practice. That does not make it late, but it means the mission setting must be evaluated.

## Limits

No loaded extant Greek Thomas witness. The phrase "Lord" needs Greek retroversion control.
