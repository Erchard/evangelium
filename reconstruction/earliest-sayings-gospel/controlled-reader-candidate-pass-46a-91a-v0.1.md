# Controlled Reader-Candidate Pass for 46A and 91A v0.1

Status: completed controlled reader pass, 2026-07-18.

## Purpose

This pass decides whether the two candidates promoted by Review C should enter the clean reconstructed reader:

- 46A: John / least-in-kingdom core.
- 91A: recognition-of-time core.

The pass does not include the full Thomas forms of Logia 46 or 91.

## Decision Summary

| Candidate | Decision | Confidence | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 46A John / least-in-kingdom core | `INCLUDE_WITH_MARKER` | Medium | Strong Matthew 11:11 / Luke 7:28 control; distinct from the already included Logion 22 children/kingdom core; printable as a compact saying without Thomas's knowledge wording. |
| Full Logion 46 | `KEEP_APPENDIX_ONLY_FOR_NOW` | Medium | The Adam-to-John frame and "know the kingdom" wording may be Thomasine or interpretive; no extant Greek Thomas witness is loaded. |
| 91A recognition-of-time core | `INCLUDE_WITH_MARKER` | Low-medium to medium | Strong Luke 12:54-56 / Matthew 16:2-3 control; compact and distinct from Logion 5; printable without the christological dialogue frame. |
| Full Logion 91 | `KEEP_APPENDIX_ONLY_FOR_NOW` | Low-medium | The "tell us who you are so we may believe" opening is a later-looking dialogue frame and should not be printed in the clean reader. |

## Reader Change

The clean reader expands from 34 to 36 logia/cores.

New clean-reader units:

- Logion 46:

> Серед народжених жінками немає більшого за Йоана; але найменший у царстві більший за нього.

- Logion 91:

> Ви розпізнаєте вигляд неба й землі, але не розпізнаєте цей час.

## Rationale

Logion 46A is included because it preserves a clear John-the-Baptist boundary saying. Logion 22 already carries the children/kingdom motif, but it does not carry the John/kingdom contrast. Therefore 46A adds a distinct early tradition rather than duplicating Logion 22.

Logion 91A is included because the recognition-of-time rebuke is compact and strongly controlled. It does not duplicate Logion 5: Logion 5 concerns hidden/revealed material, while 91A concerns failure to interpret the present time.

Both units are included only with apparatus markers. Neither has an extant Greek Thomas witness in the loaded corpus. Their Greek layer is therefore `Greek retroversion, hypothetical`, controlled by canonical Greek parallels and the Coptic witness.
