# Controlled Clean-Reader Candidate Pass v0.1

Status: completed pass.

Date: 2026-07-17.

## Purpose

Split-Core Decision Reviews A and B identified several possible additions to the clean reader. This pass decides which candidates actually improve the reconstructed earliest sayings gospel.

The rule for this pass is strict:

> A candidate should enter the clean reader only if it is compact, non-duplicative, adequately controlled, and printable without large speculative reconstruction.

## Promoted Reader Additions

This pass adds three units to the clean reader:

- Logion 45: grapes/thorns and figs/thistles core.
- Logion 47: two masters core.
- Logion 63: rich-fool core.

The clean reader now contains 34 logia or cores.

## Non-Promoted Candidates

The remaining candidates stay in the full appendix and evidence apparatus. This is not a rejection of their historical interest. It is a decision that they are not yet strong enough, clean enough, or non-duplicative enough for the first reader layer.

## Greek Policy

No newly promoted unit has a loaded extant Greek Thomas witness. Their Greek layer must therefore be printed only as:

`Greek retroversion, hypothetical`
