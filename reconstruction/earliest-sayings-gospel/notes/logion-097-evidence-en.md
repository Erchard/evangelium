## Rationale

This parable may be an independent Thomas parable: a woman loses meal from a jar without noticing and arrives home to find it empty. It is narratively strong but lacks close external controls.

Its interpretive potential is high, but reconstructive confidence is low-medium at best. It belongs in the appendix as uncertain independent parable material.

## Appendix Use

Use it to explore possible meanings: unnoticed loss, hidden depletion, empty arrival, kingdom as fragile possession, or critique of unawareness.
