- Internal controls: Logia 80 and 111 for world/body/world-unworthy language.

## Rationale

Logion 56 is compact and memorable: knowing the world means finding a corpse, and the world is not worthy of the one who finds it. Its force is unmistakable, but its evidence is almost entirely internal to Thomas's symbolic world.

The world-as-corpse motif may be a key to Thomas interpretation, yet it is not presently secure as an earliest recoverable gospel unit.

## Appendix Use

Use the saying in the appendix to explain Thomas's negative valuation of the world and its relation to life/death imagery.
