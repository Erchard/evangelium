## Decision

Current publication decision: `DEFER_TO_CLUSTER`.

## Source Basis

- Coptic base: extant in NHC II,2.
- P.Oxy. status: no loaded Oxyrhynchus Greek witness.
- Greek policy: Greek retroversion is hypothetical.
- Internal controls: Logia 63 and 110 for wealth, power, and renunciation.
- Canonical/thematic control: Luke 12:16-21 only as a broad wealth warning, not a direct wording control.

## Rationale

The saying is short but paradoxical: the rich should reign, and the powerful should renounce. It could be ironic, eschatological, or a Thomas renunciation saying. Current evidence does not decide among those readings.

Because external control is weak, it belongs in a wealth/renunciation cluster.
