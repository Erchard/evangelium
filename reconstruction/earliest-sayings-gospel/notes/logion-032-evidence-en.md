# Logion 32 Evidence Note

Status: evidence note v0.1.

## Decision

## Working Core

> A city on a high mountain cannot be hidden.

Marked fuller form:

> A city built and established on a high mountain can neither fall nor be hidden.

## Main Reason

Logion 32 has a strong evidence profile:

- complete Coptic Thomas;
- strong Greek P.Oxy. 1 witness;
- close synoptic control in Matthew 5:14.

The visible city on a mountain is short, memorable, and strongly paralleled. Thomas/P.Oxy. 1 has a fuller form with "built" and "established/fortified" and with the additional claim that the city cannot fall. This may preserve an independent fuller form, but it may also be an expansion.

## Reconstruction Implication

Add Logion 32 to the Ukrainian reader with a marker. The city-on-mountain core is strong; the "built/established/cannot fall" shape remains marked.
