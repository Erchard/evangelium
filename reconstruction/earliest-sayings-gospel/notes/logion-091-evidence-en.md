# Logion 91 Evidence Rationale v0.1

## Decision

Current publication decision: `DEFER_TO_CLUSTER`.

Reader implication: do not include Logion 91 as a full saying in the clean reconstruction.

## Source Basis

- Coptic base: extant in NHC II,2.
- P.Oxy. status: no loaded Oxyrhynchus Greek witness.
- Greek policy: hypothetical retroversion; Greek loanwords in Coptic do not equal a preserved Greek witness.
- Canonical controls: Luke 12:54-56 and Matthew 16:2-3 for recognizing sky/earth and the time.

## Rationale

The second half has a strong synoptic control: people read the face of sky and earth but fail to recognize the time. The opening request, "Tell us who you are so that we may believe," is a christological dialogue frame.

The recognition-of-time subunit may be important, but the full logion should be deferred until the dialogue frame and controlled core are separated.

## Appendix Use

Use Logion 91 to explain split-unit reasoning: a strong controlled subunit can exist inside a composite Thomas dialogue.
