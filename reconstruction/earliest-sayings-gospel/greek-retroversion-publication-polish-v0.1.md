# Greek Retroversion Publication Polish v0.1

Status: completed, 2026-07-18.

## Purpose

This pass makes the Greek layer safe for publication-facing use. Its aim is not to reopen clean-reader membership, but to prevent a reader from confusing a manuscript witness with a scholarly Greek retroversion.

## Scope

Checked the current 37-unit clean reader across:

- `reconstructed-gospel-greek.md`
- `parallel-edition.md`
- `greek-retroversion-confidence-audit-v0.1.md`
- `evidence-dossier-en.md`
- `project/open-task-prompt-queue-2026-07-18.md`

## Result

Clean-reader membership did not change.

The Greek layer now uses four normalized publication statuses:

- `Extant Greek witness`
- `Partial extant Greek witness`
- `Lacunose extant Greek witness`
- `Greek retroversion, hypothetical`
